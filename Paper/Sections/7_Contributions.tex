\section{Contributions}

Mathew did research into \glspl{zkp} and implemented \enquote{cheat mode} for our Battleship implementation. He wrote the majority of \cref{sec:methodology} of this paper.

Sangtani created the different Circom circuits used for benchmarking and recorded the results used in \cref{sec:results}. In addition, he implemented Merkle trees for board commitments. Additionally, he wrote \cref{sec:introduction,subsec:circomsnarkjs} of this paper.

Siegel implemented the first na{\"\i}ve circuit made by Sangtani into the Battleship game, allowing the later circuits to be easily integrated. He also wrote \cref{sec:relatedWork,sec:discussion,sec:conclusion} of this paper.

Vestlund created the base Battleship game and later implemented the final na{\"\i}ve and Merkle tree circuits made by Sangtani. Furthermore, he wrote the abstract, \cref{sec:results}, \cref{sec:acknowledgements}, and also assisted in writing \cref{subsec:hiddenInformationGames,subsec:merkle,subsec:zkFriendly,subsec:gameSequence}.
